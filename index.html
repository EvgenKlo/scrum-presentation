Scrum values

ARTIFACTS OF SCRUM

Product Backlog

The Product Backlog is an emergent, ordered list of what is needed to improve the product. It is the single source of work undertaken by the Scrum Team.

Sprint Backlog

A sprint backlog is a list of work items your team plans to complete during a project sprint. These items are usually pulled from the product backlog during the sprint planning session.

Increment

Increment is a specific step towards achieving the Product Goal. Each Increment is addition to all the previous ones. They are carefully checked to ensure joint work of all Increments. In order to provide value, Increment must be suitable foruse.

Scrum process

Scrum technology is suitable for the rapid development of new products. It allows you to combine specialists from different departments into one team and set a common goal for them. Progress in the group is faster due to well-established interaction, a small number of participants and a common interest in performance.
Scrum is a project management method for companies where there are opportunities and resources for experimentation. If everything goes strictly according to plan, there is a clear deadline and budget, Scrum loses its meaning.
Another detail, without which it is impossible to fully use the method, is the willingness of the customer to take part in the development. Not everyone is interested in this and can find the time.
